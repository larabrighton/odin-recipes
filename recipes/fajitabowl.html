Fajita Bowl

>

Description

This smoothie is delicious and highly customizable. Try swapping the kale for spinach, the hemp seeds for cashews or almond butter, or the frozen banana for frozen cauliflower.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken
  • avocado oil
  • salt
  • garlic powder
  • chili powder
  • cumin
  • 1 lime
  • 60g farro
  • kale
  • cotijo cheese
  • bell pepper
  • white onion
  • 1 small avocado
  • salsa

Steps

    To marinate chicken:
  1. Combine avocado oil, salt, garlic poweder, chili powder, cumin and lime juice in a bowl
  2. Trim and butterfly chicken, add it to bowl and use tongs to coat chicken with marinade liquid
  3. Marinate in the fridge for 2-3 hours
    4. To cook:
  4. Heat large grill pan over high heat for 2-3 minutes
  5. Fill small saucepan with water and set over high heat
  6. Measure farro and rinse in a fine mesh sieve under cold water
  7. Once grill pan is hot, add half the chicken and set a timer for 5:30 minutes
  8. Once water is boiling, add a pinch of salt and reduce heat to medium
  9. Add farro to boiling water, stir well, and set a timer for 21 minutes
  10. When chicken timer goes off, flip chicken and set timer for another 5:30 minutes
  11. When first batch of chicken is done cooking, set aside on a cutting board to rest
  12. Cook second batch of chicken same as the first
  13. Heat a small sautee pan over medium heat and add 1 tbsp avocado oil
  14. Chop about 1/3 bell pepper and 1/4 white onion
  15. Add pepper and onion to pan with avocado oil and sautee, adjusting heat so veggies don't brown
  16. When farro timer goes off, strain out the water and set aside
    17. To plate:
  17. Add kale to bottom of bowl
  18. Add farro
  19. Add 2 tbsp cotijo cheese
  20. Add chicken and veggies
  21. Add avocado
  22. Add 2 tbsp salsa
