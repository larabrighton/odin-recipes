Pasta with Sausage and Broccolini

>

Description

Use any type of short dried pasta and try different veggies like bell pepper, tomatoes or asparagus

Ingredients

  • 90g pasta
  • salt
  • olive oil
  • garlic
  • red pepper flakes
  • 5oz sweet Italian sausage
  • broccolini
  • parmagiano reggiano

Steps

  1. Add olive oil to pan
  2. Cook sausage
  3. Boil water
  4. Add broccolini, garlic, and red pepper flakes to pan
  5. Turn down heat and cover/li>
  6. Add pasta to boiling water
  7. When pasta is done, transfer to pan with a little pasta water
  8. Cook 1 minute then transfer to bowl
  9. Add lots of parmagiano reggiano
