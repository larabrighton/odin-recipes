Smoothie

>

Description

This smoothie has 35g of protein and only 650 calories

Ingredients

  • 350g water
  • 30g hemp seeds
  • 30g kale
  • 200g greek yogurt
  • 80g frozen banana
  • 150g frozen strawberries
  • 150g frozen mixed berries

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to blender
  2. Blend on low for 30 seconds
  3. Blend on high for 2-3 minutes until smooth
